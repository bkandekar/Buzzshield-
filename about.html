How Phototaxis Outsmarts Chemical Warfare

For millions of years, nocturnal biting insects have used thermal cues and UV radiation gradients from cosmic bodies to navigate. Our engineers calibrated a proprietary 365nm UV neon-photon wave that perfectly matches their biological sensory peaks.

Once the mosquito approaches the wavelength to investigate, it is swept quietly into our internal 1200V-2000V safe electrical grid. Death is physical, instantaneous, and painless.

More importantly: because our trap emits zero aerosols, toxic smokes, or micro-particulates, your room's Air Quality Index (AQI) remains perfectly clean, ensuring deep, uninterrupted, asthmatic-safe sleep all night.
